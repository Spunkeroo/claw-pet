Best Pet Products & Gadgets of 2026: Top Picks for Dogs & Cats

By claw.pet | March 1, 2026 | 8 min read

Smart Pet Tech

Automatic pet feeders with app control (PetSafe, PETLIBRO) ensure consistent feeding schedules even when you're away. Pet cameras (Furbo, Petcube) let you watch, talk to, and toss treats to your pet remotely. GPS trackers (Fi, Apple AirTag on collar) — never lose your pet again. Smart water fountains — cats especially prefer running water, and smart models filter and track consumption. AI pet health monitors — devices that track activity, sleep, and eating patterns to detect health issues early.

Best Dog Products

Elevated dog beds keep pets cool and off hard floors. Kong Classic — the indestructible toy that keeps dogs occupied for hours when filled with treats. Slow feeder bowls prevent bloating in fast eaters. No-pull harnesses (Ruffwear Front Range) — better than collars for walks. Dog DNA tests (Embark, Wisdom Panel) — discover your dog's breed mix and genetic health risks. Interactive puzzle toys reduce boredom and destructive behavior.

Best Cat Products

Cat trees and towers — vertical space is essential for cat happiness. Self-cleaning litter boxes (Litter-Robot 4) — expensive but life-changing for cat owners. Interactive laser toys that run on timers for play while you're away. Window perches — cats love watching the world outside. Feliway diffusers — pheromone diffusers that reduce stress and behavior problems. Subscription boxes (KitNipBox, Meowbox) make great monthly gifts.

Health & Wellness

Pet insurance (Lemonade, Healthy Paws) — covers unexpected vet bills that can reach $5,000-10,000. Dental chews — dental disease is the #1 health issue in dogs and cats. Joint supplements with glucosamine for older pets. Calming treats with CBD or L-theanine for anxiety. Grooming tools — the Furminator deshedding tool is a must for shedding breeds. Regular grooming at home saves $50-100 per professional grooming session.

Where to Shop

Chewy — the best overall pet retailer with fast shipping and excellent customer service. Amazon — widest selection and Subscribe & Save for recurring items (food, litter). Petco/PetSmart — good for in-store pickup and vet services. BarkBox/Meowbox — monthly subscription boxes with curated toys and treats. Check claw.pet for deals and recommendations.
