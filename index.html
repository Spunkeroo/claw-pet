Pet Care Quick Tips

💧

Fresh Water Daily

Change your pet's water bowl at least once a day. Clean bowls prevent bacteria buildup.

🏃

Daily Exercise

Dogs need 30-60 min of exercise daily. Cats need 15-20 min of active play to stay healthy.

🦷

Dental Health

Brush your pet's teeth 2-3 times per week. Dental disease affects 80% of dogs by age 3.

💉

Regular Vet Visits

Annual checkups catch problems early. Senior pets (7+) should visit the vet twice a year.
